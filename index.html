AKIBA STUDIOS is a lifestyle brand founded in Nairobi, Kenya in 2021 that bridges bespoke craftsmanship with ready-to-wear streetwear collections. Akiba Studios has established a distinctive identity by creating powerful designs that forge new African narratives through their unique aesthetic approach across fashion, fine arts, film, and interactive media. Their consistent and immediately recognizable visual language is cultivated through intentional branding and compelling storytelling that spans multiple creative disciplines.

AKIBA'S WORLD

Navigate on an epic adventure through Akiba's World! Navigate through challenging terrains and obstacles as he embarks on a quest to find the elusive Studio 18 Rave. Collect special items, solve puzzles, and discover hidden secrets along the way.

Developed by: Akiba Studios
Game Design: Akiba Team

RUNWAY RUSH

In Runway Rush, you're not just driving a Matatu; you're transporting the latest fashion trends to the most anticipated photoshoot of the season. Navigate through the Nairobi streets, avoiding boddas and other mats, to ensure your models arrive on time and in style. Collect fashion items and bonuses along the way to upgrade your nganya and unlock new outfits for your models. Will you make it to the shoot before the jam?

Developed by: Akiba Game Studios
Lead Designer: Akiba Team

STYLE SWING

Become the ultimate fashion golfer in this stylish and fast-paced game! Compete against the computer or challenge friends in multiplayer mode to see who can score the best while looking their best. Will you be the most fashionable golfer on the course?

Developed by: Akiba Interactive
Art Direction: Akiba Design Team
